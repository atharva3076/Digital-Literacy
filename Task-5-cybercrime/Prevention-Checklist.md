#### **Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist**





* Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, passwords, or bank details with anyone.
* Avoid clicking on unknown or suspicious links received via SMS, email, or social media.
* Always verify the identity of unknown callers before trusting them.
* Use strong and unique passwords for all your accounts.
* Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) for better security.
* Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or sensitive activities.
* Never approve UPI collect requests from unknown people.
* Remember: You do NOT need to enter your UPI PIN to receive money.
